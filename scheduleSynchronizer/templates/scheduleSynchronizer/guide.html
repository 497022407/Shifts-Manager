Instruction

  1. Sign in your Microsoft account on the right top of the page.
  2. Click on "Shift" at the top of the page to manage your schedules.
  3. Please be patient for a moment while the Shifts manager checks the consistency of all the schedules in your Microsoft Teams Shifts.
  4. To synchronize the schedules in your Microsoft Shifts, please name the group name to the exact course name listed on the course planner.
  5. To search for a specific course detail, please input the exact course name listed on the course planner.
